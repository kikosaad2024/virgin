Graduate English Students Association

Who are we? What are we planning?

Welcome! Our website is designed as a resource for graduate students in UVA’s English department. Want to learn about upcoming events? How about finding information on specialized reading groups? Couldn’t make the last GESA meeting, but want to find out what happened from the minutes? It’s all here. We also have an extensive database of past orals lists for students taking their third-year exams. Take some time to look around and explore. Also, please contact GESA President Jack Bradford if you have any questions.
